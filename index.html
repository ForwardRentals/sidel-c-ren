Questions, answered

The short version.

Do we have to replace our boilers?

No — that's the point. The Sidel SRU condenser retrofits onto the boilers and furnaces you already run, so you start recovering heat without ripping out existing equipment.

How much can we actually save?

It depends on the building, but high-volume sites typically cut energy waste by up to 40%. Every engagement starts with a free audit, so you get a real number rather than a guess.

What exactly is a "thermal battery"?

The Novacab TESS unit stores heat instead of electricity, using a Synthetic Phase Change Material (SPCM) as a thermal shock-absorber. You charge it with recovered heat, then draw clean, dispatchable power back out on demand.
